About Me---

Olaniran Samuel staged name Ritchey is a Nigerian born 3-octave-range voice coach and wedding singer He got certified in music rudiments and voice training from the Elaborate Music Institute, Lagos and Art of Singing, Ibandan. The mass-communicator has shared stages with Timi Dakolo, Yinka Davies, and Ego Egbaro(of lagbaja's band). Part of his voice coaching clientele has been TV hosts, band singers, fashionisters etc spanning across Uganda, Ghana, Hiata, South African amongs others. In 2019 he coached a singer who in turn clinched in the grand prize winning title of a Christ Embassy music competition. With his sonorous and captivating voice, he sings for couples as they walk down the aisle, at numerous wedding receptions, for proposals and birthday suprises. He is a talent to watch out for.
